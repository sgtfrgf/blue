School Activities

At Blue Bell School, we believe in a well-rounded education that goes beyond academics. Our students participate in a variety of activities that promote creativity, teamwork, and personal growth.

  • Sports: We offer a range of sports activities, including football, basketball, and badminton.
  • Arts: Our arts program includes music, dance, and visual arts, allowing students to express their creativity.
  • Clubs: We have various clubs, such as the Science Club, Debate Club, and Eco Club, where students can explore their interests.
  • Community Service: We encourage students to give back to the community through volunteer work and service projects.
  • Events: Throughout the year, we organize events like cultural festivals, science fairs, and sports days to celebrate our students' achievements.

H O U S E S

The House System at Blue Bell School is designed to foster a sense of community and competition among students. Our school has a House System that promotes teamwork, leadership, and a sense of belonging among students. Each student is assigned to one of four houses, and they participate in various inter-house competitions throughout the year.

  • Red House: Known for its spirit and enthusiasm.
  • Blue House: Recognized for its academic excellence.
  • Green House: Celebrated for its sports achievements.
  • Yellow House: Valued for its creativity and arts.

Each house competes in sports, academics, and cultural events, earning points for their achievements. The house with the most points at the end of the year is awarded the House Cup.
